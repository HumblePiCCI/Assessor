The annual science fair took place at Northview Secondary School on Thursday afternoon. Students presented projects in the gym, and families visited to ask questions and vote for their favorites.

Projects included model bridges, water filters, and experiments about plant growth. "I wanted to test which soil helped plants grow faster," said Grade 9 student Amina Hassan. Judges looked at creativity, results, and presentation.

Teacher Mr. Lewis said the event helps students practice research and communication skills. Winners received certificates and small prizes. The school plans to display some projects in the library next week.
