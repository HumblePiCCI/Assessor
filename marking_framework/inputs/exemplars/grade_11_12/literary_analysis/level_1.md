In "Glass Orchard" the narrator goes back to the old house and thinks about the past. There is an orchard that used to be nice but now its broken. The theme is memory because the narrator remembers stuff and feels sad.

The narrator says "the trees looked like they were made of glass" which means they are fragile. Also the dad is mentioned and there is regret. In the end the narrator leaves and thats the point that you cant go back.

This story shows memories can hurt and you should move on.
