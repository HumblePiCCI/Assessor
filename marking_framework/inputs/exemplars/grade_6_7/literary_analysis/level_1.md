In "The Last Lap" Maya runs a race and she falls and then she feels bad. She was mad at the other girl because she bumped her but it was also Maya fault because she was not watching. The theme is like consequences, because when you do something you get something back.

Maya makes a mistake when she tries to cut in and she trips. Then she gets consequence because she loses the race. "I hit the track hard" shows she fell. After that she says she will do better next time. The coach helps her and thats good.

This story teaches you dont cheat and dont give up. If you do a mistake you will pay for it.
