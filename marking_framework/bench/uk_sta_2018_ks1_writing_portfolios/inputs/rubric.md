
# Rubric

This dataset uses the STA end of key stage 1 teacher-assessment framework.

## Working towards the expected standard
- Writes sequenced sentences that form a short narrative.
- Demarcates some sentences with capitals and full stops.
- Represents spoken words with plausible grapheme choices in some words.
- Spells some common exception words.
- Forms lower-case letters and uses spacing between words.

## Working at the expected standard
- Writes simple, coherent narratives and records real events simply and clearly.
- Demarcates most sentences with capitals and full stops; uses question marks when required.
- Uses present/past tense mostly correctly and consistently.
- Uses coordination and some subordination to join clauses.
- Spells many words and many common exception words correctly.
- Forms capitals/digits appropriately and spaces words proportionately.

## Working at greater depth within the expected standard
- Writes effectively and coherently for different purposes.
- Makes simple additions, revisions, and proofreading corrections.
- Uses KS1 punctuation mostly correctly.
- Spells most common exception words and many suffixed words correctly.
- Uses some joins in handwriting.
