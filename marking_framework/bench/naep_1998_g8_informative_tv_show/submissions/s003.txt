Dear Mr. President,

I think you should have a show about “Women in history.” A lot of people want to know about women and what they’ve done to help our country. There have been many women heroes, and they should be recognized. You could do the show like Wishbone, except all the shows be about women in history instead of characters from a book. An idea for a show is Anne Frank. You could go to the place where they hid for so long and do the show right there. Everyone will get the chance to see how Anne lived. A lot of people haven’t heard or seen her story. Well, it’s time they do! So, please take into consideration my ideas and respond when you make your decision.
