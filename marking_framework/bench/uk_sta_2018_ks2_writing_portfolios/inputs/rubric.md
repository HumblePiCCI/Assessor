
# Rubric

This dataset uses the STA end of key stage 2 teacher-assessment framework.

## Working towards the expected standard
- Writes for a range of purposes.
- Uses paragraphs to organise ideas.
- In narratives, describes settings and characters.
- In non-narrative writing, uses simple structural devices for the reader.
- Uses core punctuation mostly correctly.
- Spells most year 3/4 words and some year 5/6 words correctly.
- Writes legibly.

## Working at the expected standard
- Writes effectively for a range of purposes and audiences.
- Describes settings, characters, and atmosphere in narratives.
- Integrates dialogue to convey character and advance action.
- Selects vocabulary/grammar mostly appropriately for the task.
- Builds cohesion within and across paragraphs.
- Uses tense and taught KS2 punctuation mostly correctly.
- Spells most year 5/6 words correctly and maintains joined legibility.

## Working at greater depth within the expected standard
- Writes effectively for a range of purposes and audiences, selecting language to manage impact on the reader.
- Distinguishes between spoken and written language choices.
- Exercises assured control of register.
- Uses a range of clause structures and cohesive devices.
- Uses punctuation precisely to enhance meaning.
