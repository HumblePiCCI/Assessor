Rubric (Open Response)

Level 4 (80-89)
- Clear, focused main idea
- Specific and relevant supporting details
- Strong explanation linking details to the claim
- Well organized and controlled conventions

Level 3 (70-79)
- Clear main idea
- Relevant supporting details
- Adequate explanation
- Organized writing with some minor convention errors

Level 2 (60-69)
- Main idea present but general
- Some relevant details
- Limited explanation
- Some organization and noticeable convention issues

Level 1 (50-59)
- Main idea unclear or weak
- Few relevant details
- Minimal explanation
- Limited organization and frequent convention issues
