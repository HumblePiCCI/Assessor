Assess writing on a 0-100 scale (Ontario aligned).

Criteria:
- Ideas and Content: clear opinion, relevant reasons, useful details.
- Organization: intro, body, conclusion, logical sequencing.
- Voice and Word Choice: clarity, variety, appropriateness.
- Conventions: spelling, grammar, punctuation, sentence control.

Level bands:
- Level 1: 50-59
- Level 2: 60-69
- Level 3: 70-79
- Level 4: 80-89
- Level 4+: 90+
