Ontario-style Persuasive Writing Rubric (0-100 mapped to levels)

Level 4+ (90-100)
- Insightful, precise claim; compelling reasoning and evidence
- Strong organization with smooth transitions
- Purposeful voice and audience awareness
- Very strong control of conventions

Level 4 (80-89)
- Clear, focused claim with strong support
- Effective organization and cohesion
- Appropriate style and vocabulary
- Minor conventions errors only

Level 3 (70-79)
- Clear claim with adequate support
- Generally logical organization
- Sufficient clarity and control
- Some conventions errors that do not impede meaning

Level 2 (60-69)
- Claim present but general or inconsistent
- Uneven support and organization
- Limited precision in language
- Noticeable conventions issues

Level 1 (50-59)
- Weak or unclear claim
- Minimal/irrelevant support
- Limited organization and clarity
- Frequent conventions issues that hinder readability
