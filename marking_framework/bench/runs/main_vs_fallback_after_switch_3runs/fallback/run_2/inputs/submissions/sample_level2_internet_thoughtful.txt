Vancouver is where I live. I think it is a good city because there are places to go and things to do.

First, there are parks and beaches. People can walk there and play games. Also in winter people can go skiing.

Second, there are many restaurants. You can eat different food from different countries. This is nice for families.

Third, there are festivals and events in summer. People can listen to music and have fun.

So Vancouver is a good city and I like living here.
