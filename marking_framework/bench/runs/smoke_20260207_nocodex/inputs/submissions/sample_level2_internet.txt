Dear Principal,

I am writing to ask you to start a recycling and cleanup program at our school. We have many students and a lot of garbage at lunch. This makes the hallways and yard look messy.

First, recycling bins should be put in every hallway. Many students throw paper and bottles in regular garbage because recycling bins are not close. If bins are easy to find, more students will recycle.

Second, classes could take turns doing a short cleanup in the school yard once a week. This would teach responsibility and help keep our space clean.

Third, we should have posters that explain what can be recycled. Sometimes students are confused and put the wrong things in bins.

If we do these three things, our school will be cleaner and students will learn better habits.

Sincerely,
Student
