# Ontario Writing Rubric (Condensed)

Assess writing on four categories (0-25 points each):

1. Ideas and Content
- Clarity and relevance of main idea
- Supporting details and explanation

2. Organization
- Clear introduction, body, conclusion
- Logical sequence and paragraphing

3. Voice and Style
- Audience awareness and tone
- Word choice and sentence variety

4. Conventions
- Spelling, grammar, punctuation, capitalization
- Sentence completeness and readability

Level guide:
- Level 1 (50-59): limited effectiveness
- Level 2 (60-69): some effectiveness
- Level 3 (70-79): considerable effectiveness
- Level 4 (80-89): high degree of effectiveness
- Level 4+ (90+): exceptional insight and control
