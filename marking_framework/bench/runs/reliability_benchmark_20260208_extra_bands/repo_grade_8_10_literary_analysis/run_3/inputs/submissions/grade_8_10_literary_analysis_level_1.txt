In the story "The Borrowed Name" its about a kid named Jordan that uses a fake name online. They do it because they think it is safer and also they want people to like them. The theme is that you should be yourself because lying causes problems.

Jordan talks to people in a forum and says their name is "J." Then they meet someone from school and it gets awkward. Jordan feels nervous and then they tell the truth at the end. "My real name felt heavy in my mouth" shows they were scared.

This story shows you shouldnt lie. If you lie you will have consequences.
