Assignment: Persuasive/Argument Writing (Grade 8-10)

Task:
Write a persuasive piece that clearly states a position on a real issue, supports that position with relevant reasons and evidence, and addresses audience concerns.

Success criteria:
- Clear arguable claim
- Relevant reasons and supporting evidence/examples
- Logical organization and transitions
- Appropriate tone and word choice for audience
- Control of conventions (grammar, punctuation, spelling)
